About AMUHacks 1.0

AMUHacks 1.0 is a Student-focused Virtual Hackathon, intending to encourage and provide a platform for students to showcase their innovative ideas and solutions in developing projects through programming and latest technologies.

In light of the COVID-19 outbreak, we’re taking our hackathon virtual! Your well being is important to us. This means that you can participate and connect right from where you are. With innovative tracks based on the need of the hour, AMUHacks 1.0 implores all the aspiring strategists to share their vision with the community. If you haven’t participated in any hackathon previously and are looking out for the opportunity to do so, you’re in the right place.

We welcome all newcomers and veterans alike. With the added benefit of being guided by mentors and honorable jury, all participants will also receive the participation certificates.

Event Details

AMUHacks 1.0 will be hosted online on Microsoft Teams on 13th September 2020. The event will commence from 10:00 AM with an introduction on Microsoft Teams. So you need to have Microsoft Teams application either in your phone or you can directly access it on the web through your PCs.

We will have the first idea and project overview submission from 11 AM onwards. Our Volunteers will contact your Team leader individually by video call and will ask some basic questions regarding your project idea. Here you need to give just a quick overview regarding what you are going to build and on what technologies. According to these inputs mentors will be assigned to your team, whom you may contact and take guidance for your project.

The first Screening cum Evaluation round will start at 5:00 PM. Here the major aspects of evaluation will be:

  • In what way will the Project impact our Society?
  • Is the Project Idea Feasible?
  • Can I get the Right Human Resources to implement it?
  • Will the Product be something that users cannot do without?
  • Will the Product be Perdurable?

The second and the final screening will begin at 7:00 PM. Here more focus will be on your Problem Statement, Presentation and the proposed Solution. Codes, Models, Software are welcome but more emphasis is laid on the innovative idea you come up with for your problem statement.

Results will be declared after the final evaluation gets completed .i.e. after 9:00 PM. The two teams successfully presenting their solutions/ software/ model in the best possible way will be declared as winners of the AMUHacks 1.0 and will receive the Winning Certificates and Prizes.

FAQs

When and where will AMUHacks 1.0 be hosted?
AMUHacks 1.0 will be hosted online on Microsoft Teams and is scheduled on 13th September 2020 from 10:00 AM – 9:00 PM IST.

Who can participate in AMUHacks 1.0?
Anyone! Whether you are a total beginner or a tech wizard, it doesn’t matter. Everyone is welcome to participate!

What are the requirements to be able to participate?
There are no specific requirements for participation. Anyone who is interested can participate. You just need to register through online form.

What is the theme?
It is an open themed hackathon. However, we will have a few tracks to push you in a direction if you and your team can not come up with an idea.

How many members can a team have?
A team should have 1-3 members.

Is there any Participation Fee?
It is FREE! Participating in AMUHacks 1.0 will cost you nothing.

Will I get a Participation Certificate?
Yes! You will receive a Participation Certificate on your Active Participation throughout the Hackathon.

What if I am a beginner?
Interest in learning and working with technology is much more important than your current experience level. We’ll have a team of mentors and volunteers to help and guide you throughout the event.
